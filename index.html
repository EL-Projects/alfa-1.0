Oksana Lukjanska

About

I love to paint pictures, especially flowers, they inspire me, their gradient and color amaze me, I love to talk about art and inspire people to paint canvases, so I have been working as an artist-teacher for 13 years with children and adults, I think up interesting lessons for learning, taking into account wishes and interests. I sell my paintings, I write to order. Education and training, work: 1999-2004 Drahomanov State University, She studied with V.L. Levchenko, Ya. A. Monin, Obukhevich L.H. In 2005-2006, she finished courses in computer graphics, worked as a designer of exhibition stands, then printing design 2010-2014 teacher at the Revutsky House of Creativity in Bucha
